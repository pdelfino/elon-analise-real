## About

Professor Elon Lages Lima's Real Analysis books are among the most widely used mathematics textbooks in Brazilian universities. While the books include an answer key, many exercises lack solutions, and those that exist are often extremely terse.

A mathematician named **Rodrigo Carlos Silva de Lima** produced an outstanding and comprehensive set of solutions to these books. He generously shares his work for free on his personal blogs. However, this material has historically been hard to find -- scattered across paywalled platforms like *Passei Direto* and *Ebah*, often requiring registration or payment to download, and available in multiple inconsistent versions (2012, 2015, 2017).

This repository exists to provide a **single, reliable, and free pointer** to these solutions, ensuring that students can find them without friction.

## Alternative Recommendations

If you are studying Real Analysis for the first time, Elon's books may not be the best starting point. Consider these alternatives:

- **[Introduction to Analysis -- Arthur Mattuck](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Analysis-Arthur-Mattuck/dp/1484814118)** -- Written by an MIT professor who taught Real Analysis to engineering students. Accessible and built from years of teaching experience. A great first read, though the exercises may be on the easier side for proof-heavy courses.

- **[Analysis I -- Terence Tao](https://www.amazon.com/Analysis-Third-Texts-Readings-Mathematics/dp/9380250649)** -- Written by one of the greatest living mathematicians. Elegant, rigorous, and remarkably clear. Tao takes a non-classical approach, carefully constructing number systems from the ground up. Used in Honors-level courses at several American universities. The exercises are challenging.

- **[Understanding Analysis -- Stephen Abbott](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9781493927111)** -- Another excellent and well-regarded textbook for learning Real Analysis.

Este repositorio existe para ser um **ponto unico, confiavel e gratuito** de acesso a essas resolucoes, garantindo que qualquer estudante consiga encontra-las sem dificuldade.

### Recomendacoes Alternativas

Se voce esta estudando Analise Real pela primeira vez, os livros do Elon podem nao ser o melhor ponto de partida. Considere estas alternativas:

- **[Introduction to Analysis -- Arthur Mattuck](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Analysis-Arthur-Mattuck/dp/1484814118)** -- Escrito por um professor do MIT que lecionou Analise Real para alunos de engenharia. Acessivel e construido a partir de anos de experiencia em sala de aula. Otima primeira leitura, embora os exercicios possam ser faceis demais para disciplinas com enfase em demonstracoes.

- **[Analysis I -- Terence Tao](https://www.amazon.com/Analysis-Third-Texts-Readings-Mathematics/dp/9380250649)** -- Escrito por um dos maiores matematicos vivos. Elegante, rigoroso e notavelmente claro. Tao adota uma abordagem nao classica, construindo cuidadosamente os sistemas numericos desde o inicio. Usado em cursos de Honra em diversas universidades americanas. Os exercicios sao desafiadores.

- **[Understanding Analysis -- Stephen Abbott](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9781493927111)** -- Outro excelente livro-texto, muito bem avaliado, para aprender Analise Real.
